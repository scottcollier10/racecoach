Welcome to RaceCoach.net

Where Champions Train

Racing is more than just going fast—it’s about control, repeatability, and racecraft. At RaceCoach.net, coaching is tailored to your car, your tracks, and your goals.

Founder and chief instructor Dave Scott has decades of experience in professional and club racing. That real-world background fuels a coaching program built to make you faster and safer on any track, in any car—not just in one combo you’ve memorized.

Whether you’re preparing for your first HPDE, dialing in race pace for a championship run, or learning how to get more out of your data and video, the goal is simple: turn your time at the track into measurable improvement.

Why Train with Us?

Coaching Built Around Real Drivers

No cookie-cutter programs or generic advice. Every driver gets a focused plan based on where they are now and where they want to go.

  • Pro-Level Experience – Coaching informed by years in pro and top-level club racing.
  • Customized Programs – Plans built around your goals, experience, and current lap data.
  • Data-Driven Improvement – Video and telemetry to prove you’re getting faster, not just feeling quicker.
  • Race-Proven Techniques – Braking, rotation, traffic management, and decision-making under pressure.
  • One-on-One or Team Coaching – Work with Dave directly or bring him in to support your whole program.
